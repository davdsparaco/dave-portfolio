fonolux

sound absorbing lamp

2012

Project developed in function of the rotomolding technique. The principal aim was not only to create an “empty inside” object, rather give a purpose to the void itself. That’s why I designed a faceted lamp full of little holes intended for the use inside empty and reverberating environments.
The edges ensure the sound waves to break against them. The holes let the waves enter the object transforming them into heat.
In this way the lamp can guarantee a reverb reduction acting as a sound absorbing element.
