alto adige - il prezzo della qualità

self publishing project

2011

it’s a self publishing project on cultural identities. Carnies, bakers, butchers, street artists, boxers, dog owners, jugglers…how many different identities are living in the same city? As group of students we tried to answer tese questions through a publishing project. It offers the possibility to have a look at the portrait of the social reality and the cultural development of the city of Bolzano.
