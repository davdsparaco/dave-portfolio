About

me

Hi, my name is Davide Sparaco, I am an Italian product and graphic designer. I moved to Berlin after graduating from the Free University of Bolzano because I was given the opportunity to work for a company, which made it possible to combine both my product and graphic design skills. I've always been interested in different materials and the science/art of what I could create with them. I started my career in high school studying furniture design, as well as designing buildings and interiors. 

experience

during my studies I started approaching the design process, beginning with the conceptualisation and ending with the concrete production of my projects. The main feature that characterises me is the hands-on mentality that I also deepened during a period of study at the Bauhaus Universität Weimar. I can identify my design approach in 4 consecutive steps: research, creation, development and communication.

work

in the last years I have had the opportunity to work in different design fields, passing through pure technical jobs as geometer, during which I improved my CAD and technical drawing skills. After that I worked as UI/UX intern in Hamburg for the company Syneo Srl, for which I developed graphics for mobile applications and designed solutions for multimedia exhibits. The most recent employment concerns product and graphic design for Miasa GmbH in Berlin. During my time at Miasa I developed Packagings, CI material, adv, POS material and I also accomplished tasks as photographer.
